SECTION 5 â€” EXACT FORMULAS (CANONICAL)
================================================================================

Invoice tax (default GST path):
  taxable   = max(0, subtotal - discount)
  tax       = round(taxable * 0.18)
  cgst/sgst = tax / 2   (or igst = tax when IGST mode)
  total     = taxable + tax + roundOff

Exact total override (QA / explicit breakdown):
  When caller supplies total + (cgst|sgst|igst), those values are stored as-is.

Payment aggregation:
  paidAmount = Î£ amount of completed payments for invoiceId
  remainingAmount = max(0, total - paidAmount)

Invoice status derivation (non-draft, non-cancelled):
  paidAmount <= 0                 â†’ sent (or overdue if dueDate < today)
  0 < paidAmount < total          â†’ partially_paid
  paidAmount >= total             â†’ paid

Client financials:
  revenue     = Î£ total where status === paid
  outstanding = Î£ (total - paidAmount) where status âˆˆ {sent, partially_paid, overdue}

Dashboard KPI outstanding:
  Same formula as client outstanding, summed across all invoices.

Accounting system journals (no double-seed):
  Invoice (non-draft/cancelled): Dr Accounts Receivable / Cr Professional Fees (total)
  Payment (completed):           Dr Bank / Cr Accounts Receivable (amount)
  Manual journals: Debit total must equal Credit total or post is rejected.

Trial Balance: Î£ Debits = Î£ Credits
Balance Sheet: Assets = Liabilities + Equity (equity is balancing figure)
